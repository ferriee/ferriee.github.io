What lessons can we learn from esports events marketing?

Based on research completed for the University of Plymouth, 03/03/2020

As esports gets bigger, it clearly gets more recognition from the ‘normal’ marketing scene. I have never worked in an established marketing scenario, but I can almost guarantee that their main couple of questions will be, what the hell is esports and can we use anything?. Seo (2013), coined the lessons that could be learnt from esports, as forms of ‘potential experiential marketing’. This is the reference that I based my research on, this article will simply be a synopsis of this piece of research, to hopefully answer the question, what the hell can we learn from esports marketing?
